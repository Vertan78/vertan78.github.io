our story

born from a love of color, joy, and really good gifts.

delighted opened its colorful doors in March 2024 on Haywood Street in downtown Asheville, and the community fell in love instantly. We believe ordinary life can be made a bit more joyful with practical items that are beautiful, whimsical, or funny.

Every item on our shelves is carefully hand-picked — from playful jellyfish doormats and hand-painted plates to cheeky tea towels, artisan candles, and locally sourced souvenirs. If it makes you smile, it belongs here.
